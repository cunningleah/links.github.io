Months after their Heidis restaurant burned down, Stewart and Heidi Woodman plan to re-open the restaurant in a new Minneapolis location, moving the eatery from its former 50th Street and Bryant Avenue location to the citys Lyn-Lake neighborhood, according to a published report.
