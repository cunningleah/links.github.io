rawdog is an RSS (and other) feed aggregator, based on Mark Pilgrim's flexible feed parser. It's just an aggregator; it's not a weblog authoring tool, nor is it an NNTP gateway, outliner, mailserver or anything else.  raw dog probably only runs on Unix-like systems.
