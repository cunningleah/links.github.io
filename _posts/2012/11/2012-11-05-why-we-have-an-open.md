Last year, we wrote a post titled "Why We Need An Open Wireless Movement." Today,EFF is proud to announce the launch of the Open Wireless Movementlocated at openwireless.orga coalition effort put forth in conjunction with nine other organizations: Fight for the Future, Free Press, Internet Archive, NYCwireless, the Open Garden Foundation, OpenITP, the Open Spectrum Alliance, the Open Technology Institute, and the Personal Telco Project.
