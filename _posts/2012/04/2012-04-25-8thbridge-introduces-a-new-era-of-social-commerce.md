Wade Gerten, Founder and CEO of 8thBridge, has spent the past year developing the platform entitled Graphite. The software-as-a-service business model will operate on a monthly fee basis. It will allow fashion and luxury brands to leverage social media without giving up brand control. It will also be integrated with existing channels and, for the first time, users can create shareable experiences outside the confines of Facebook
