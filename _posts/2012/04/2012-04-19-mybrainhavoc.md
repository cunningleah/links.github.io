The time is now to tell my story of my experiences with arteriovenous malformation (AVM) and to make something private public after 11 years. Together, we we  will take this journey and explore the beginning,  middle,  and  after of two major  brain surgeries on an AVM on the fourth ventricle of my brain stem.  In addition, we will take care of a few miscellaneous items.
