Gina Trapani and Paul Ford are programmers, interface designers, authors, editors, and broadcasters. They are consistently involved in the kinds of projects that we as librarians undertake when were at our best: finding imaginative, meaningful ways to make as much information as possible widely available, easily accessible, and interesting.
