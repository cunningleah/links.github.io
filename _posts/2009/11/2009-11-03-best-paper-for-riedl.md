CSE Professor John Riedl and his student, Michael D. Ekstrand, received a best paper award for thier work "rv you're dumb: Identifying Discarded Work in Wiki Article History" at WikiSym 2009, the International Symposium on Wikis and Open Collaboration, in Orlando, Florida. Forty-five papers were submitted, of which 16 were accepted (36%), and one named Best Paper.
