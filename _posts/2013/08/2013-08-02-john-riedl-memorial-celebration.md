The Department of Computer Science and Engineering will hold a tribute to celebrate the life and accomplishments of our colleague and friend Prof. John Riedl, who passed away July 15, 2013. The tribute will be held on Friday, August 30, 2013, in the AO Johnson Room of the McNamara Alumni Center on the University of Minnesota East Bank campus, from 3:30 to 6 pm.
