Earthworm Lawn Care is the first of its kind in the Twin Cities area to specialize in eco-friendly lawn maintenance. As a woman owned and operated business with more than 15 years experience, we have seen the negative impact conventional lawn care has on our environment and well being. Our goal is to inspire people to change the way they approach lawn care, with an end result of a cleaner healthier planet for our families and pets!
