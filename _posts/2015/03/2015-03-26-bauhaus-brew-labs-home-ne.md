Welcome to Bauhaus Brew Labs! We assume you're here because you not only enjoy delicious, refreshing local beer, but you also enjoy the combination of delicious beer with your favorite tunes and über-awesome friends. Well, you've come to the right place – welcome to the family! We are a growing clan of forward drinkers who celebrate the joy of art and craft in everyday life – the Bauhaus way. Our beers are a reflection of that, and with a focus on making delicious craft lagers, we've designed our beers to do nothing less than ignite your senses and make your wilder dreams come true.*
