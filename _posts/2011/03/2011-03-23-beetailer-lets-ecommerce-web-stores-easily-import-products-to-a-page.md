Beetailer is a new service that allows ecommerce merchants to import their websites store to a tab on their Facebook Page. Users can add items to a shopping cart, and are directed back to the merchants site for checkout. Backed by Y Combinator, Beetailer includes advanced functionality such as Like-gated discounts and promotions, scheduled wall posts about products, and analytics
