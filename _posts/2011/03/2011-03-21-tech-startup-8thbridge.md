Social e-commerce startup 8thBridge Inc., which helps businesses set up storefronts within Facebook, has raised $10 million in venture capital.
Trident Capital, a Palo Alto, Calif.-based venture firm, led the round. Split Rock Partners, which led 8thBridges Series A round, also participated.
Minneapolis-based 8thBridge will use the funds to expand its operations and improve its technology, called StoreCast.
