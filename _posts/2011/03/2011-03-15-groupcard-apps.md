GroupCard is the world's leading provider of collaborative greetings and gifting. When Grandma's 100th birthday comes around and you want the whole extended family to sign a special card, there's no better service for you. And everyone can chip in to a gift card for the recipient which can turn many smaller contributions into one really great gift!
