Most businesses dont feel any loyalty toward a particular daily deal site, and over the next few years, the report concludes, daily deal sites will likely have to settle for lower shares of revenues from businesses compared to their current levels, and it will be harder and more expensive for them to find viable candidates to fill their pipelines of daily deals.
