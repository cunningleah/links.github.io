CaringBridge today announced a partnership with Medica to help people experiencing a significant health challenge stay connected to their family and friends. CaringBridge is a nonprofit providing free websites that help people easily share health news and receive support during health challenges such as cancer, premature birth and serious injury. Medica is a Minnesota-based healthcare services company providing healthcare coverage to nearly 1.6 million members.
